N/B: Please note that the packages listed below represent the structured support options available to learners

Package 1: Routine Creation (Registration)

Our Routine Creation package is the starting point for every learner at SADA. For a once‑off registration fee of R200, learners receive a personalized routine designed to fit their academic and personal needs. This 30–60‑minute session can be conducted either over the phone or face‑to‑face in Empangeni, ensuring that each learner begins their journey with a clear, structured plan. By registering, learners are officially added to our system and gain access to the academy’s support network.

Package 2: Routine + Daily Check‑ins

The Routine + Daily Check‑ins package builds on the foundation of routine creation by adding consistent accountability. For R480 initially, followed by R280 per month, learners benefit from daily five‑minute monitoring calls. These check‑ins provide encouragement, track progress, and help learners stay on course with their routines. Parents can feel confident knowing that their child is being guided and supported every day, with measurable results that reflect commitment and growth.

Package 3: Premium Support

Our Premium Support package is designed for learners who want maximum guidance and mentorship. The first month costs R650, followed by R450 per month thereafter. This package includes routine creation, daily monitoring, and weekly 30‑minute calls with Top Achievers via Teams, WhatsApp, or direct communication. Learners also receive an end‑of‑term progress review to evaluate their achievements and areas for improvement. Premium Support ensures that learners are not only disciplined but also inspired by role models who have excelled academically.

Recruiter Incentive

At SADA, we reward commitment and results. Learners who demonstrate discipline and progress can earn part‑time roles as recruiters, helping to expand the academy’s reach. Recruiters receive a 15% commission for every new learner they bring in, turning discipline into both personal growth and an opportunity for leadership. This incentive encourages learners to take ownership of their journey while contributing to the success of others.
