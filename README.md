## Project Overview
The South African Discipline Academy (SADA) was founded in 2026 in Empangeni, KwaZulu-Natal, by S'fiso Majola. It is an online academy dedicated to helping learners build discipline, structure, and consistency in their daily lives. SADA offers structured packages combining routine creation, daily check-ins, and mentorship, with a focus on credibility, measurable results, and scalable growth across South Africa.

- **Mission:** Empower learners by instilling discipline, building effective routines, and providing mentorship that improves academic performance and personal growth.  
- **Vision:** Become a leading nationwide academy recognised for transparency, credibility, and measurable impact.  
- **Target Audience:**  
  - Primary: Parents of high school learners  
  - Secondary: Learners themselves  
  - Future Expansion: Schools and communities across South Africa  

---

## Website Goals and Objectives
- Present SADA as a professional, trustworthy academy with clear offers and testimonials.  
- Explain services, pricing, and learner progress tracking in a parent-friendly way.  
- Encourage parents to register learners via online forms and contact options.  
- Serve as a hub for social media traffic and outreach.  
- Provide a parent portal for progress updates.  

**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**  
- Lead generation  
- Conversion rate  
- Website traffic  
- Engagement & retention  
- Parent feedback
