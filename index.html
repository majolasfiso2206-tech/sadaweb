Welcome to the South African Discipline Academy™ Empowering learners with discipline, routines, and mentorship for success.

SADA is a discipline and time management coaching programme designed for high school learners nationwide. After observing a lack of structured discipline among students, we decided to launch a programme that provides tailored routines, daily monitoring, and mentorship from top achievers. Our mission is to empower learners to build discipline, improve academic performance, and develop leadership skills. Registration is R200 once‑off to place learners into our system. We operate nationwide via phone calls and online platforms, with one‑on‑one home visits available for learners in Empangeni.

Mission

To empower learners by installing discipline, building effective routines, and providing consistent mentorship that improves academic performance and personal growth.

Vision

To become a leading nationwide academy that transforms learner discipline and achievement, recognised for transparency, credibility, and measurable impact.
